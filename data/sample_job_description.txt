Job Title: Sales Executive – B2B SaaS

Company: Acreva Technologies

Location: Remote / London, UK

About the Role:
We are looking for an experienced Sales Executive to join our growing team.
You will be responsible for managing the full sales cycle from prospecting to close,
working with small business owners and decision-makers in the UK and Europe.

Required Skills and Experience:
- Minimum 2 years of B2B sales experience, preferably in SaaS or tech
- Strong communication and presentation skills
- Experience with CRM tools (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive)
- Comfortable with outbound cold calling, LinkedIn outreach, and email campaigns
- Proven track record of meeting or exceeding sales targets
- Self-motivated with strong time management skills

Nice to Have:
- Experience selling to HR, recruitment, or operations teams
- Familiarity with AI tools or automation software
- Previous startup or scale-up experience

What We Offer:
- Competitive base salary + commission (OTE £50,000–£70,000)
- Fully remote with flexible working hours
- Opportunity to grow with a fast-moving AI startup
- Supportive team environment and regular training
